Splice the wire from PIN 2 and connect -v to the board and +v to the chip.
1uf (Micro Fara) 63v Electrolytic Capacitor. It is recommended that a small
amount of electrical .insulation tape be used to wrap around the mod chip
and capacitor to avoid any unwanted electrical contact with the PS2 during
installation.

The lazer might need adjusting. The pot adjustment is on the lazer unit.
There are two pot adjusters one for games and one for dvd. The you need
is the one on the left.

The best way to adjust your lazer is with a ohm meter. Adjust the pot to
600ohms.
