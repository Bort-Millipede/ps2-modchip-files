*** Clarification on Point H/Y ***


-) Point H/Y connects to Point H on the board only on NTSC consoles,
   to enable support for NTSC consoles.

-) Point H/Y connects to point Y on V9-V10-V12 boards to enable reading of -RW media

-) On V9-V10-V12 either connect H or connect Y, you cannot connect both on H/Y.
