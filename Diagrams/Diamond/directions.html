Diamond ModChip Operation

It is assumed that your machine is in the Standby mode, ie machine is not switched on

PS2 games

Insert the game into the disc tray and press Reset button.

PS1 games

Insert the PS1 game into the disc tray and press Reset button twice when the light is green.

DVD movies

Insert the DVD movie into the disc tray and press Reset button twice when the light is green.

Disable Mode

Insert the original game into the disc tray and put the PSTwo to Standby mode. ( Red Light )
Press and hold Reset for at least 2 seconds then the Diamond Chip is disabled.
The Diamond Chip is disabled until it has been put back to STANDBY and boot normally.
