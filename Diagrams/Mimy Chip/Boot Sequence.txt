▪ PS2 original/backup CD-DVD games : insert the game and press reset.
▪ PS1 original/backup games : insert game , put console on standby (red reset button light on) , turn on and hold reset button until blue led on eject button lights up.
▪ Original/backup DVD movies : insert the game , put the console on standby (red reset button light on) , turn on and hold the reset button until the blue led on the eject button turns on.
